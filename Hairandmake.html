Bridal Hair & Makeup

This project highlights our work styling the bride for her romantic garden wedding. Our team created a soft, natural look that complemented the pastel tones and rustic décor, ensuring she felt confident and radiant throughout the day.

From flawless skin preparation to intricate hair styling, every detail was carefully chosen to enhance her natural beauty while lasting through the ceremony, reception, and evening celebrations.

“The hair and makeup team made me feel like the best version of myself. My look stayed perfect all day and night, even through all the dancing!”

Sara Wilsson

Bride

Our stylists worked closely with the bride to design a look that reflected her personality and matched the overall wedding theme. The result was timeless elegance with a modern touch.

This portfolio piece demonstrates our dedication to creating bridal hair and makeup that is polished, long-lasting, and unforgettable.
